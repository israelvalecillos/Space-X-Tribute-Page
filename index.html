SpaceX

A private company whose objective is to reduce the costs of space travel and facilitate the colonization of the planet Mars

biography

SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the aim of reducing the costs of traveling to space to facilitate the colonization of Mars. SpaceX has developed several launch vehicles, the Starlink constellation, the Dragon cargo ship, and brought astronauts to the International Space Station on Dragon 2.

latest achievements

  • 2 March 2019 First private company to send a human-rated spacecraft to space.
  • 3 March 2019 First private company to autonomously dock a spacecraft to the International Space Station (ISS).
  • 25 July 2019 First use of a full-flow staged combustion cycle engine (Raptor) in a free flying vehicle. The benefit is a much longer life than conventional engines; it is expected to able to be re-used 1000 times.
  • 11 November 2019 First reuse of payload fairing. The fairing was from the ArabSat-6A mission in April 2019.
  • 30 May 2020 First private company to send humans into orbit.
  • 31 May 2020 First private company to send humans to the International Space Station (ISS).
  • 24 Jan 2021 Most spacecraft launched into space on a single mission, with 143 satellites.
